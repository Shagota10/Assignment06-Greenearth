About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change, one tree at a time. By participating in our campaign, you'll help restore forests, create natural habitats, and reduce carbon emissions. Our key goals include:

  • Reforestation of natural habitats
  • Carbon emission reduction
  • Support for local communities
